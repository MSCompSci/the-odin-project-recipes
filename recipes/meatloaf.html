Classic Meatloaf Recipe

Description:

This tasty meatloaf recipe is a quick and easy dinner for the whole family!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground pork
  • 1 cup (150g) breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup (50g) finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup (50g) finely chopped bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and ketchup. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. Shape the mixture into a loaf and place it in a greased 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together ½ cup of ketchup and 1 tablespoon of brown sugar. Spread this mixture evenly over the top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  6. Let the meatloaf rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve with mashed potatoes and steamed vegetables for a classic meal.
  • You can also pair it with a side salad or your favorite gravy.
