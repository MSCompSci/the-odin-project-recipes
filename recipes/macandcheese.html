Classic Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Macaroni:

  • 8 oz (225g) elbow macaroni
  • Water for boiling
  • Salt for boiling water

For the Cheese Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp (57g) unsalted butter
  • 4 tbsp (32g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (946ml) whole milk
  • 4 cups (450g) shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)

For the Topping (optional):

  • 1 cup (115g) breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp (28g) unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) if you plan to add a topping.
  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Add the flour to the melted butter and whisk to form a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes, whisking constantly.
  5. Gradually add the milk to the roux, whisking continuously to prevent lumps from forming.
  6. Cook the mixture over medium heat until it thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the shredded cheddar cheese. Stir until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth.
  8. Season the cheese sauce with salt, black pepper, and nutmeg (if using).
  9. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until the macaroni is well coated.
  10. If using a topping, mix the breadcrumbs and melted butter in a small bowl. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over the macaroni and cheese in a baking dish.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the edges are bubbly.
  12. Let the macaroni and cheese rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve with a side of steamed vegetables or a simple green salad.
  • You can also add cooked bacon, ham, or vegetables to the macaroni and cheese for extra flavor.
